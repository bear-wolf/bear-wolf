As staff members spoke out one by one, Wendy wrote down their
ideas on a large sheet of paper. From the brainstorming session,
Wendy derived six unique categories that many of the responses
addressed. She organized the categories on a diagram that looked

like the skeleton of a fish. At the “head” of the skeleton, she wrote
“Improved Office Morale,” as the ultimate goal. In this way, Wendy
introduced the staff to the Fishbone Diagram, a versatile tool that
is often used for brainstorming and problem root cause analysis.
